Adapted from a tale by Jay Scherick, David Ronn, Thomas Lennon, and Robert Ben Garant, Baywatch is a 2017 American action comedy film that was directed by Seth Gordon. Its script was written by Mark Swift and Damian Shannon. It is based on the Michael Berk, Douglas Schwartz, and Gregory J. Bonann television series. Zac Efron and Dwayne Johnson both appear in the movie. The plot centers on lifeguard Mitch Buchannon and his colleagues as they battle a drug lord to defend their beach.
The superhero movie Thor: Love and Thunder, released in 2022, is based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name. It is the follow-up to Thor: Ragnarok (2017) and the 29th movie in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, produced by Marvel Studios and released by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures (MCU). Chris Hemsworth plays Thor in the movie, which was directed by Taika Waititi and also features Christian Bale, Tessa Thompson, Jaimie Alexander, Waititi, Russell Crowe, and Natalie Portman. Waititi and Jennifer Kaytin Robinson also co-wrote the screenplay. When Gorr the God Butcher (Bale) tries to exterminate all gods, Thor (Bale) must go back to fighting and enlist the help of Valkyrie (Thompson), Korg (Waititi), and Jane Foster (Portman), who is now the Mighty Thor.
The 2013 Filipino Family Comedy-Drama film Four Sisters and a Wedding was helmed by Cathy Garcia-Molina. Toni Gonzaga, Bea Alonzo, Angel Locsin, and Shaina Magdayao, four of Star Cinema's most well-known young actresses, play the four sisters who are trying to prevent their younger brother, Enchong Dee, from being married in the movie. Originally a member of the main cast, actress Angelica Panganiban withdrew and was replaced by Shaina Magdayao.
The 2018 American sports drama film Creed II, which stars Sylvester Stallone and Juel Taylor, is directed by Steven Caple Jr. The movie is the eighth in the Rocky movie series and the follow-up to Creed (2015). It features Dolph Lundgren, Tessa Thompson, Wood Harris, Phylicia Rashad, Michael B. Jordan, Stallone, and others. In the movie, Viktor Drago, the son of Ivan Drago, opposes Donnie Creed (Jordan), who continues to be trained by Rocky Balboa (Stallone) (Lundgren)
The 2016 American buddy action comedy film Central Intelligence was written by David Stassen, Ike Barinholtz, and Rawson Marshall Thurber. Kevin Hart and Dwayne Johnson play two former classmates from high school who escape after one of them joins the CIA to protect the globe from a terrorist who wants to sell satellite codes in the movie.
